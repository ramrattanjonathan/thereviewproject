MELODRAMA
This is a sample review of Lorde's sophomore album Melodrama. The overall book-binding layout of this review is an attempt to recreate a design I saw on an awwwards website (root-food.com), but the designs for each individual review were designed by me. Scroll (mousewheel down/up) through the uniquely designed reviews or use click controls to navigate. Clicking on a chapter (track) will auto scroll you to the review of that track, whilst just simply hovering will reveal an image from that particular review. Clicking "Back to home" will take you back to the homepage. All images were (painfully) scoured from Genius (genius.com) or Pinterest (pinterest.com). This entire website is optimized for a 1920 x 1080 desktop on Google Chrome and most definitely will not work on mobile, but laptops and other browsers may work (not tested).
Written by: Jack Antonoff, Jakob Jerlström, Joel Little, Lorde, A Strut & Tove Lo
